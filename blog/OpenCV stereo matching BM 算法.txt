 Basically OpenCV provides 2 methods to calculate a dense disparity map:


 cvFindStereoCorrespondenceBM: Fast (can process several images per second), but if parameters not tuned then the results are poor. cvFindStereoCorrespondenceGC: Really Slow (takes several seconds, even minutes per image), but gets very accurate results.

 In this post I willfocus on cvFindStereoCorrespondenceBM, this method is based on Konolige's Block Matching Algorithm.
